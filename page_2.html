Counseling and Psychogical Services at U of M

UMSI recognizes how important mental health is in ensuring that you are thriving both in and out of the classroom.

Mental Health

Here are some services offered by CAPS to help support your mental health:

Let's Talk

“Let's Talk” is a free program that supports students starting their mental health journeys. It provides casual meetings with counselors during specific drop-in times for initial support and advice.

Peer Counseling

Sometimes there's nothing better than talking to people who know exactly what you're going through. That's where peer counseling comes in!
