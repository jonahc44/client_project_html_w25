Welcome to the UMSI Success Navigator

Your Comprehensive Guide to Resources for Academic Success

This guide is set up to help you be successful inside and outside of the classroom and we have included the following resources throughout our site:

  • Academic Resources
  • Mental Health
  • Community Resources

Mental Health:

Maintaining your mental well-being is just as important as your physical health. Here are some resources to support your mental health:

Counseling and Psychological Services (CAPS):

Access individual counseling, group therapy, workshops, and crisis intervention services.

Wellness Coaching:

Work with a wellness coach to develop strategies for managing stress, time, and overall well-being.

Mindfulness Resources:

Participate in mindfulness and meditation sessions to cultivate a calm and focused mind.

Support Groups:

Join peer support groups for shared experiences and collective coping strategies.

Academic Support:

At UMSI, we like to challenge our students to push their limits. We understand that in order to do so successfully, outside support is often needed. Here are some options available for some extra academic support:

Tutoring:

Utilize our peer tutors to help with programming or math!

Libraries:

The libraries here at U of M offer a wide breadth of resources.

Services for Students with Disabilities:

If there are any disabilities that are making your academics more challenging and less accessible, SSD has many options to support you.
